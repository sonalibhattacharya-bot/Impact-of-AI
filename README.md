Independent Variable
1.	Task Automation (Perceived level of AI doing the work)
A significant portion of my daily work is handled by automated systems	AI completes many of the tasks I used to do manually	I rely on AI-based tools for most parts of my workflow	Much of my decision-making is influenced by AI outputs
							

2.	Pace Automation (AI increasing pressure/speed/tempo)
AI tools accelerate the overall pace of my work	Automation increases the urgency with which I must complete tasks	AI-driven timelines reduce my time for reflection and quality checks.	Automation creates pressure to respond immediately
3.	Surveillance / Algorithmic Monitoring
I feel I am constantly being observed through digital systems	I worry about making mistakes because automated systems track everything

Mediator Variables
1.	Human–AI Collaboration Quality
I find it easy to coordinate my tasks with AI systems.	AI enhances, rather than replaces, my judgment	I am able to integrate AI inputs smoothly into my workflow.
		
2.	Perceived Job Security / Job Insecurity
My organization may reduce human roles because of automation	I worry that my job may become obsolete due to automation.	Automation increases my concerns about layoffs.
		
3.	Trust in AI / Technology Trust
I rarely question the outputs produced by AI.	I feel confident using AI tools in critical tasks
	
4.	AI Literacy / Employee Confidence Using AI (also used as moderator)
I understand the limitations and potential errors of AI.	I have the skills needed to work with AI technologies.

Moderator Variables
1.	Leadership Style (Supportive/Ethical/Digital Leadership)
My leader explains clearly why new technologies are introduced.	Leaders actively help us learn how to use AI tools	Managers reduce uncertainty by communicating openly about AI
		
2.	Organizational Digital Maturity / Readiness
My organization has strong digital systems and infrastructure	My organization invests in upskilling employees for AI	AI adoption is planned carefully rather than rushed
		
Dependent Variables

1.	Employee Well-Being
I feel positive about my work most days.	I experience good work–life balance	I feel enthusiastic when starting work each day.	I feel that my work environment supports my well-being
